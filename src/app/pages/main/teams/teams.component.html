Meet our CEO

Elsie Nwaise, she is passionate about customer service delivery and has over 15years experience in organizational and strategic leadership in operations, finance and customer care from the banking industry.

She has had the responsibility of training many diverse teams. She is a leader, dynamic and highly focused individual. After working in the United Kingdom for a couple of years, she returned to Nigeria where she realized that there was a great need in the environment for proper customer care and etiquette.

Elsie holds an MBA degree from Southampton Solent University, UK and a M.SC degree in Environmental toxicology and Pollution Management from the University of Lagos, Nigeria.
